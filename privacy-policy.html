Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how TREMENDO ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information from and about you when you use our TREMENDO application ("App").

What Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services to you. The types of information we collect include:

Information you provide to us: This is information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, or any other information you enter or upload into the App.

Information we collect automatically: When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage. This information may include:

Device and browser information: Such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

Usage information: Such as the features you use in the App, the time, date, and duration of your activity, and other usage statistics.

Information from third parties: We may also collect information from third-party services, such as social media platforms or other APIs, if you connect those services to our App.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

To provide and operate the App and its services.

To personalize your experience and remember you for future use.

To improve the App and develop new products and features.

To communicate with you, including sending you promotional emails or updates if you have opted in.

To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity.

How to Request Deletion of Your Data

If you wish to request the deletion of your personal data, please send an email to contacto@tremendo.online with the subject "Data Deletion Request." We will process your request in accordance with applicable laws and our data retention policies.
